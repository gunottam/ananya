## üë®‚Äçüç≥ THE TUESDAY "ONE POT" RECIPE
*Takes 15 mins. Cook enough for Lunch AND Dinner.*

1. **Prep:** Soak 50g Soya chunks in hot water (5 mins), squeeze hard. Cube 200g Paneer.
2. **Heat:** Oil + Ginger-Garlic Paste + 1 chopped Onion + 1 chopped Tomato.
3. **Spice:** Salt + Turmeric + **Kitchen King or Paneer Masala**.
4. **Mix:** Throw in Soya and Paneer.
5. **Cook:** Stir fry for 5 mins. Add a splash of water to make it semi-dry (lat-pata).
6. **Done.** Divide in two: half for Tiffin, half for Dinner.
